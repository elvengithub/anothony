# Section Structure (in order)

1. **`#hero`** — Full-screen background image, animated zoom, name + tagline, two CTA buttons, stats block bottom-right, scroll indicator bottom-left.
2. **`#about`** — Two-column grid: framed photo placeholder (left) + bio text with achievement tiles (right).
3. **`#expertise`** — 3×2 CSS grid of hover-reveal cards, each with a background image, overlay, number, title, and description.
4. **`#listings`** — Horizontal scroll row of property cards. Each card has: image with badge, price, name, location, specs (beds/baths/sqft), CTA link.
5. **`#testimonials`** — 3-column grid of quote cards with star ratings, italic quote text, avatar, name, and role.
6. **`#media`** — Centered "As Seen On" logo text row + featured video embed placeholder with play button overlay.
7. **`#resources`** — 3-column grid of resource cards (book, course, report) with image, type tag, title, description, CTA.
8. **`#contact`** — Two-column: contact details (left) + lead capture form with name/email/phone/select/textarea/submit (right).
9. **`#social`** — Centered row of 4 social platform cards (Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube, Twitter/X).
10. **`#footer`** — 4-column grid: brand + tagline + social icons, Services list, Markets list, Quick Links. Bottom bar with copyright + legal.
